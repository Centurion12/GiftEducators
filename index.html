What is Gift of the Educators about?

Gift of the Educators is a None Profit Organisation dedicated towards helping students in improverished areas, that have limited access to quality education facilities.This is accomplished by offering Tuition services for both the Students that can afford the hourly fees and for those that cannot.

How does the Gift of the Educators Organisation work?

  1. None Profit:
    • Meaning, there is no finantial gain for any of the Organisations members
    • Any money that is left over from the Organisations expenses get put back into the Organisation
  2. What expenses does the money cover?
    • The money that is made from the Tuition lessons, is then injected back into the Organisation to allow less fortunate Students the oppertunity to gain access to the Tuition lessons for free
    • The Organisation also has to maintain General Expenses
  3. How does the Organisation decide on whether you would need to pay or whether you don't?
    • The Organisation basis most of this decision making on a basic test that is compulsory if you feel that you can not afford the tuition lessons
    • Please click HERE to do the test
  4. How does the Organisation get Tutors?
    • Because the Organisation is a None Profit Organisation, all tutors are expected to be voluntary, however all tutors are qualified
